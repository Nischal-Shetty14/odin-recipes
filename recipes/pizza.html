Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a yeasted flatbread topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. It's baked in an oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings: bell peppers, onions, olives, mushrooms, etc.
  • Salt and herbs (oregano, chili flakes)

Steps

  1. Mix yeast, water, and a pinch of sugar. Let it sit for 10 minutes to activate.
  2. Add flour, salt, and oil. Knead into a soft dough and let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll the dough into a circle. Spread pizza sauce over it.
  4. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
  6. Remove, slice, and enjoy hot!
