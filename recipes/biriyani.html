Biriyani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with layers of basmati rice, marinated meat and a rich blend of spices. It's slow-cooked for a delicious, tender finish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or vegetables
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala (or mix of spices)
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt and oil/ghee

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes. Cook it until 70% done and set aside.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, biryani masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt for 30 mins.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown. Remove half for layering later.
  4. Cook the marinated chicken with tomatoes and fried onions until tender.
  5. In a pot, layer rice and chicken, sprinkle saffron milk, mint, and fried onions.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Gently mix and serve hot with raita or salad.
