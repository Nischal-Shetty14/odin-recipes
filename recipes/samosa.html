Samosa

Description

Samosa is a crispy and spicy Indian snack made by stuffing a flaky pastry with a savory potato filling. Itâ€™s deep-fried to golden perfection and enjoyed with chutneys.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Water (for kneading)
  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • Spices: cumin seeds, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder
  • Salt and oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, oil, and a pinch of salt. Add water and knead into a stiff dough. Rest for 30 mins.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, green chili, mashed potatoes, peas, and spices. Mix well and cool.
  3. Divide dough into balls, roll each into a circle, and cut in half.
  4. Form a cone with each half and stuff with the potato filling.
  5. Seal the edges with water.
  6. Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with green chutney or ketchup.
